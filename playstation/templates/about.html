About Trophy Hunter

As an avid trophy hunter, I found myself often consulting between my playstation account trophies, various game rating sites, trophy blogs, and even a person Excel Spreadsheet I made for my hunt. I found this all to be quite cumbersome and I decided to make a webapp that will help fellow trophy hunters find their prey.

The site uses the PlayStation API to find public trophy information, and compares it with a playstation game database built from various web sources. Together, the app will recommend the next games for you to play, and as a member, it will maintain an up-to-date list of great PlayStation ripe with trophies.
